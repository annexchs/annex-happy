What is Happy Appy?

It’s just what it sounds like – a daily app that will make you smile. Tickle your fancy. Bring a smile to you on a mediocre day. We like using Happy as a proper noun – let’s call it a destination. A place where, for just a minute or two every day, you can visit laughter.

Every day, Happy Appy will load a new, funny video onto your iOS device. It’s that simple. A video that’s never rude, crude, or nude. It’s just – Happy.

For free. Try it now.

The Inspiration

Rusty. A dog.

Every day, when I grab his leash, Rusty is happy. Joyful. Every day. No matter what.

Isn’t this what we all ought to aspire to? Moments of happiness, every day?

It’s hard to do, sometimes. In a world where the headlines that dominate our news are mostly depressing, anxiety can weigh heavy on our hearts. We forget to laugh. Often, we feel that nothing is funny. Nothing can break us out of our funk.

When I had cancer I felt that way. I needed something to break me out of that very dark place, and I found myself searching for You Tube videos to make me laugh. There was so much excellent, honest footage of laugh-out-loud moments, I was hooked.

And that’s when I conceived of the idea for Happy Appy. Just tap the icon, and feel the joy of Rusty heading out to pee on some trees.

About the Videos

The truth is, the sweet things that make us laugh are often things that happen without intention. You’ll see a lot of animals, a lot of babies and kids…and that’s because there’s just something about the naivety of those creatures that make their actions especially hilarious.

You’ll also see some ads and commercials along the way, and I wish there was something I could do to prevent that. Ads rarely make me happy. But I don’t make any of the videos that you’ll see in Happy Appy – I just direct you to them. So sometimes you’ll see an ad at the beginning of a video, or possibly a pop up. All I can tell you is this…If there’s an ad it makes me sad, but Happy’s worth the wait. (And isn’t that why the Mute button was invented?)

We love for people to send us links to Happy videos that we haven’t featured. Got one? If you’ve got our premium app, you can send us potential Happys. The submissions can be done on this site or through the app directly. We hope that you’ll respect our promise for videos that are never crude, rude or nude. We’ll delete anything inappropriate and remove you from our submission list. Which will make you Unhappy.

Frequently Asked Questions about Happy Appy

What’s the difference between the Free version and the Premium version – besides 99 cents?
The Free version lets you watch the day’s Happy video when you touch the icon.

The Premium version lets you easily Share videos with friends, save videos to Favorites, set a Happy Alarm, Rate videos, store your Peeps (those people you send videos to often), send Birthday videos and Submit your own videos for consideration. Not bad for .99, huh?

Why are there ads on some of the videos?
Unfortunately, there’s nothing we can do about this. YouTube is in charge, here. We just access footage from their site.

Do you make any money on the videos that are shown?
Nope. Every video belongs to the person who posted it. Happy Appy simply redirects App users to those videos.

I just watched a Happy Appy video that I’d like to share with a friend. Can I send it to them through email?
Our Premium version allows users to easily send videos to any of their contacts. Videos can be sent via email or text – you choose.

How do I set the alarm feature?
Turn on the alarm button by touching the ON/OFF button in the top right corner of the Set Alarm screen. Then select a time and a sound. The alarm is set! (but must be reset each day).

Can I send you a video that I’ve made?
Absolutely, if you have the Premium version. But you’ll have to post the video to You Tube first. Then send us the link through our web site or our app by touching the Got Happy? button in the bottom right corner of the App.

How do I save my favorite videos?
With the Premium version, simply touch the star with the plus sign, in the upper right corner of your screen, after watching the video that you’d like to save.

I saved a video to Favorites, but it’s no longer there. What happened?
Sometimes content on You Tube is omitted by the person who posted it, or by YouTube. Happy Appy can’t control that.

Will I ever see the same video twice?
Not if we can help it…(unless you’ve saved it to Favorites)

Is your company affiliated with YouTube?
Nope. We just go to them for content.

How can I contact customer service?
Please email us at customerservice@myhappyappy.com.
